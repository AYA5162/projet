With your Family

How to build a healthy relationship with family

*Spend quality time with all your family members

*Communication is the key to understand each other and solve any conflicts you might face

*Gratitude and appreciation because not worshipping family and taking for granted is something you would always regret

*Last and most important is respecting one another

With your Friends

Keeping a healthy friendships is challenging but it's not impossible,here are some points that will help down that long alley:

*Support, trust and honesty should always be mutual

*Listen to your friends cause friendship require attention and knowing the need of your friend

*Avoid the judgment and accept your friends the way they are, unless they bring toxic energy and negativity in your life

*Don't talk behind your friends back

*Respect your friends and their boundaries and everything they stand for a, except if it's a source of harm wether for you or them

*Always forgive and seek forgiveness when you are wrong ,at the end of the day we are all humans

With Strangers

Believe In your self worth , this is very important point because it will showcase people how much you love and value yourself which will make them respect you as who you are, besides having a positive fulfilling self image is more likely to attract positive relationships.

*Being kind and compassionate towards people and try to focus on the positive qualities rather than the perceived flaws

*Don't let people pass your boundaries or ignore your values
